Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Summary: LiveHeavy is a content publishing operation. We do not collect, store, or sell personal data from viewers. We use third-party platform APIs solely to publish content to our own social media accounts.

1. Who We Are

LiveHeavy ("we," "us," or "our") operates an automated video content channel that publishes short-form clips to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. Our website is located at https://consolejb820-sketch.github.io/liveheavy-site-/. You can contact us at consolejb820@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

LiveHeavy does not operate a user-facing application that collects personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information from website visitors or content viewers.
  • We do not require account registration on our website.
  • We do not use tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or behavioral analytics on our website.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Automatically Collected Information

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URL. This data is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement. LiveHeavy does not have direct access to this server log data.

4. Use of Third-Party Platform APIs

LiveHeavy uses official APIs provided by TikTok, YouTube (Google), and Meta (Instagram) exclusively to publish video content to our own accounts. We do not access, read, or store any user data from these platforms.

  • TikTok Content Posting API: Used solely to upload and publish video content to our own TikTok account. We access only the permissions necessary for content publishing and comply with TikTok's Privacy Policy and TikTok's Platform Terms.
  • YouTube Data API: Used solely to upload videos to our own YouTube channel. Complies with Google's Privacy Policy and YouTube API Terms of Service.
  • Meta Graph API (Instagram): Used solely to publish video content to our own Instagram account. Complies with Meta's Privacy Policy.

API credentials and access tokens are stored securely in encrypted environment variables on our private servers and are never shared or exposed publicly.

5. Cookies

Our website does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking, or analytics.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data from visitors, there is no personal data to retain or delete. API access tokens are retained only as long as the associated platform accounts remain active.

7. Children's Privacy

LiveHeavy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at consolejb820@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.
